Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the personal information you provide when using our Resume Builder.

2. Data Collection & Temporary Storage

Our Service is designed with privacy in mind. We do not store your personal resume data in a persistent database. The information you enter is processed temporarily in your browser and on our servers.

When you generate a PDF or preview, temporary files (such as generated PDFs or uploaded profile images) are stored securely on our servers for up to 7 days to allow you to download them. After this period, they are automatically permanently deleted.

3. Third-Party Services & Data Processors

To provide our services, we utilize trusted third-party providers:

  • OpenAI (AI Features): If you use the "AI Enhanced" feature, your text inputs are securely transmitted to OpenAI via their API for rewriting. OpenAI acts as a data processor and, per their enterprise API policies, does not use your data to train their public AI models.
  • Cloudflare Turnstile (Security): We use Cloudflare to protect our servers from bots and spam. Cloudflare may process your IP address and browser telemetry to verify that you are a human.

4. Advertising and Cookies

To keep this Service free, we display third-party advertisements. These third-party ad networks (e.g., Google) may use cookies, web beacons, or local storage to collect non-personal data about your browsing activity to serve personalized ads.

We also use your browser's Local Storage to save your UI preferences (like Dark Mode) and temporarily hold your resume data so you don't lose your work if you refresh the page. This Local Storage data never leaves your personal device.

5. Sharing of Information

Because we do not store your personal data persistently, we do not sell, rent, or share your resume information with third-party marketers. Data is only shared with the essential technical processors mentioned in Section 3, or if required by law enforcement.

6. Security

We implement standard security measures to protect the temporary processing of your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. You acknowledge that you submit sensitive career information at your own risk.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@buildmyairesume.com.
