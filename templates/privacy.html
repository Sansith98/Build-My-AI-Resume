Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the personal information you provide when using our Resume Builder.

2. Data Collection and Storage (No Persistent Storage)

Our Service is designed with privacy in mind. We do not store your personal resume data in a persistent database. The information you enter (such as your name, contact details, education, and work history) is processed temporarily in your browser and on our servers solely to generate your final document.

Any temporary files (such as generated PDFs or uploaded profile images) are stored only for a brief period to allow you to download them and are automatically deleted from our servers shortly after.

3. Third-Party Data Processing (OpenAI)

If you choose to use the "AI Enhanced" feature, the personal and professional data you input will be securely transmitted to OpenAI via their API. This is strictly for the purpose of analyzing, rewriting, and formatting your resume content. By selecting the AI Enhanced option, you consent to this transmission.

OpenAI acts as a data processor. According to their API policies, data sent to their API is not used to train their foundational AI models.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We may use your browser's Local Storage to save your UI preferences (such as Dark Mode settings) and temporarily hold your resume data so you don't lose your work if you refresh the page. This data remains on your device and is not collected by us.

5. Sharing of Information

Because we do not store your personal data, we do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third-party marketers or advertisers.

6. Security

We implement standard security measures to protect the temporary processing of your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@buildmyairesume.com.
